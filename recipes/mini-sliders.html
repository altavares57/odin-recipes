Mini Sliders

Bite-sized burgers for the win.

What's better than a fresh burger? Five. That's what. Lets not waste any more time; we both know what you're here for.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb of ground beef
  • 12 dinner rolls
  • onion soup mix
  • 2 cups of shredded cheese
  • 1/4 cup of mayonnaise
  • A lot of sliced pickles
    •

    Steps

    1. Preheat oven (350 degrees F).
    2. Mix beef and onion soup.
    3. Cook and stir until beef is browned. Stir in cheese and mayo.
    4. Put your freshly prepped beef + cheese combo on bottom portion of dinner roll.
    5. Put top of dinner roll on beef + cheese to make a slider
    6. Put them in the oven for about 30 minutes.
    7. Add pickles. Lots of them.
    8. Eat way too many sliders and induce gastrointestinal distress.
    9. Fin.
