Fancy Grilled Cheese

Feelin' fancy?

Consider some freshly melted coagulated milk on a grilled bread of your choosing. With some bread, butter, cheese, a skillet, and a stovetop, your tummy will rumbly no more. If you're wondering what makes this particular recipe fall into the fancy umbrella, its a secret.

Let's get started:

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Butter
  • cheese
  • tomato*optional*
    •

    Steps

    1. Preheat skillet to medium to medium/low heat.
    2. Put butter on one side of sliced bread.
    3. Place buttered side on preheated skillet.
    4. TOP IT WITH CHEESE, and another side of bread, buttered side out.
    5. cook until browned to your satisfaction, flip, repeat.
    6. And finally, top with some fresh parsley. Fancy
    7. Enjoy it, but don't burn your mouth!
